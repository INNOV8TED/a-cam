Character Import Guide

For the cleanest A-CAM composites and AI generations, prepare your character image using these best practices before uploading:

✅ DO
  • Solid Backgrounds Use a transparent PNG, pure white, or green screen background.
  • Full Body Framing Ensure the head, arms, and feet are fully visible in the frame.
  • Flat Lighting Studio or neutral lighting works best. A-CAM will add the cinematic shadows for you.
❌ DON'T
  • Cluttered Backgrounds Avoid cityscapes, rooms, or props touching the character.
  • Cropped Images Don't use waist-up or headshots. They will look unnatural and float.
  • Harsh Shadows Avoid images where the subject is already half in pitch-black shadow.
